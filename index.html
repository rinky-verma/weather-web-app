In scientific terms, both weather and climate are about atmospheric conditions like temperature, precipitation, and other factors. But they differ in scale. Weather involves the atmospheric conditions and changes we experience in the short term, on a daily basis.Rain today, sun tomorrow, and snow next month- thats weather.This is often understood to mean 30 years or more.

What is Weather?

Weather refers to short-term atmospheric conditions-the temperature and precipitation on a certain day, for example. climate refers to the average atmospheric conditions that prevail in a given region over a period of time-whether a place is generally cold and wet or hot and dry,for example.
